EasyCounter Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

EasyCounter (the "App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

On-Device Storage (Not Sent to Our Servers)

  • Session records (exercise type, count, peak BPM, duration, date/time)
  • Language preference
  • Advertising tracking consent

These are stored in iOS UserDefaults and never transmitted to our servers.

Camera Input (Real-Time Processing)

  • Camera is used only for detecting exercise movements.
  • Frames are processed in memory in real time and are never saved or transmitted.

Third-Party SDK (Google AdMob)

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA) — only if you consent via the App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Device info (model, OS version, language)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

2. How We Use the Information

  • To provide app functionality (counting, session records)
  • To personalize user experience (language, ad relevance)
  • To improve stability and performance

3. Data Retention

On-device data is retained until you delete the app or select "Reset All Records" in the app's settings.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data is only shared with Google AdMob for ad delivery.

5. Your Rights

  • Camera permission: can be revoked anytime in iOS Settings
  • Ad tracking consent: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Delete records: Reset All Records in the app's Settings

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

Changes will be announced in the app or on this page.
